Recipe

Corn and capsicum skewers with lime

1 tablespoon sea salt 2 teaspoons grated lime rind 1/2 teaspoon dried chilli flakes 1 tablespoon sea salt

Method

1. Place corn on a large, heatproof microwave-safe plate. Add 2 tablespoons cold water. Cover. Microwave on high (100%) for 4 minutes or until just tender. Set aside for 10 minutes to cool. Pat dry paper towel. Trim ends of corn cobs. Cut each cob into 3 rounds.

2. Using a small sharp knife, cut around stem of 1 capsicum. Remove and discard. Using a teaspoon, remove and discard seeds and membrane. Repeat Corn and capsicum skewers with lime

3. Remove and discard. Using a teaspoon, remove and discard seeds and membrane. Repeat with remaining capsicums. 3 Thread corn and capsicums alternately onto skewers. Place on a baking tray. Spray with oil.

4. Combine lime, chilli flakes and salt in a small bowl. Heat a barbecue plate or chargrill on medium heat. Cook skewers, turning, for 5 to 6 minutes or until vegetables are tender. Transfer to a plate. Sprinkle with lime and chilli salt. Serve.
